Churros

Description

Churros are long, slender pastries with a ridged surface, resembling a straightened-out doughnut or a long, thin tube. They are typically crispy on the outside and soft and doughy on the inside.

Churros are a popular Spanish and Portuguese dessert that has also gained popularity in many other parts of the world.

  • Water: 1 cup
  • Unsalted butter: 1/2 cup (4 ounces)
  • Granulated sugar: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • All-purpose flour: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 3 large eggs
  • Vegetable oil: For frying
  • Granulated sugar: For coating (about 1/2 cup)
  • Ground cinnamon: For coating (1-2 teaspoons, to taste)
