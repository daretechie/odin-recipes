Mexican Egg Bake

Description

A Mexican Egg Bake, often referred to as a Mexican-inspired breakfast casserole or egg dish, combines traditional Mexican flavors and ingredients into a hearty and satisfying dish.

ingredients

  • Eggs: Typically 6-8 large eggs, beaten.
  • Onion: 1/2 cup finely chopped onion.
  • Tomatoes: 1 cup diced tomatoes (fresh or canned).
  • Jalapeños (optional): 1-2 jalapeños, seeded and finely diced for added heat.
  • Chorizo or Ham: 1 cup cooked and crumbled chorizo sausage or diced ham.
  • Cheese: 1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend (such as cheddar, Monterey Jack, and/or queso fresco).
  • Tortilla Chips or Tortillas: 1 cup crushed tortilla chips or torn corn tortillas.
  • Milk or Cream: 1/2 cup milk or cream (to add creaminess to the egg mixture).
  • Spices and Seasonings:
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh Cilantro: 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro, plus more for garnish.
  • Salsa: For serving on the side, if desired.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. Spread cooked chorizo or ham in the baking dish. Layer diced bell peppers, onion, tomatoes, jalapeños, and crushed tortilla chips or torn tortillas on top.
  3. Whisk together eggs, milk or cream, ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the layered ingredients in the baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle shredded Mexican cheese on top. Bake for 25-30 minutes until eggs are set and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro. Serve warm, optionally with salsa on the side.
