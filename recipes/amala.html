Amala

Description

Amala is a versatile and nutritious food ingredient derived from the mature roots of the African yam bean (Sphenostylis stenocarpa).

It is widely consumed in West Africa, particularly in Nigeria and Ghana, where it holds cultural significance and is used in various culinary dishes.

ingredients

  • Yam Flour:Depend on the volume
  • Water: The amount of water used can vary depending on the desired texture of the Amala

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Prepare Yam Flour
  3. Mixing
  4. Kneading using wooden spatula or spoon
  5. Forming Amala
  6. Serving with Ewedu and Gbegiri
